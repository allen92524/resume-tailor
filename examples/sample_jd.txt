Senior Backend Engineer — DataFlow Inc.

About Us:
DataFlow is a fast-growing data infrastructure company helping enterprises manage and analyze their data at scale. We're looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to join our platform team.

Responsibilities:
- Design and build scalable backend services that process millions of events per day
- Own the architecture of our data pipeline infrastructure
- Write clean, well-tested code in Python and Go
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams including product, data science, and DevOps
- Participate in on-call rotation and incident response
- Mentor junior engineers and contribute to engineering best practices

Requirements:
- 5+ years of professional software engineering experience
- Strong proficiency in Python; experience with Go is a plus
- Experience building and operating distributed systems at scale
- Deep knowledge of SQL and NoSQL databases (PostgreSQL, Redis, DynamoDB)
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS preferred)
- Experience with containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Strong communication skills and ability to work in a collaborative environment

Nice to Have:
- Experience with data streaming technologies (Kafka, Kinesis)
- Familiarity with infrastructure-as-code (Terraform, CloudFormation)
- Contributions to open source projects
- Experience with event-driven architectures

We offer competitive compensation, equity, flexible work arrangements, and a culture that values learning and growth.
